学んだこと

What We Learned

MMDA monitors 500+ CCTV cameras in real-time — incidents are detected within seconds and response teams dispatched instantly across the metro.

The command center also handles flood monitoring using water level sensors across Metro Manila's critical waterways and pumping stations.

BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) contributes roughly 9% of Philippine GDP, employing 1.3 million+ Filipinos serving clients worldwide.

Microsourcing lets global companies build dedicated offshore teams in the Philippines — combining world-class skill with cost-effectiveness.

A "smart city" isn't just tech — it's the people behind the screens making fast, life-saving decisions with the data in front of them.
