学んだこと

What We Learned

UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems are the silent heroes keeping hospitals, data centers, and telecom networks alive during outages.

OpenText manages information lifecycles for thousands of companies — from creating a document to archiving it decades later, all AI-assisted.

Power engineering isn't just about electricity — it's about reliability, redundancy, and ensuring critical systems never go dark.

Enterprise software like OpenText's serves as the "memory" of a corporation — organizing vast amounts of data into searchable, secure knowledge.

Both companies showed us that IT careers in the Philippines are not just available — they're globally competitive and well-compensated.

振り返り

Reflection

"Visiting two very different yet complementary industries in one day was eye-opening. From physical power hardware at Hytec to digital information management at OpenText — I realized every technological advancement needs both reliable power AND reliable data. These are two invisible pillars of modern civilization."
